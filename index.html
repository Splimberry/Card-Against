Game Basics

Round flowA question appears, everyone answers before the timer ends, then answers are graded and points resolve.
Answer rulesAliases and minor typos can count if the intended answer is clear.

ScoringYou will gain a small bonus for getting as accurate as possible. Difficulty, streaks, and active modifiers can boost your point earned.
Table eventsSome rounds add global rules adding a fun twist to the round.
AchievementsAchievement can only be earned/progressed in public 10-round matches, bot rounds and local 1v1 will not contribute to unlocking achievements.
